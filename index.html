HELLO!

My name is Brooks Susor. This is my humble blog.

This is a tech-focused blog with an emphasis on anything and everything web development. My goals with this blog are to:

  • Share what I know to help others interested in web development and tech.
  • Reinforce my own understandings of the things I have learned along the way.
  • Provide myself with a creative outlet that I have been missing since college.

I must say, I am in the early stages of creating this blog. I am also in the early stages of my career as a developer. I will convey the information that I have learned to the best of my ability. However, I may not fully understand a concept. I may phrase something incorrectly. Please take everything that I write with a grain of salt.

I hope the topics I choose to write about are beneficial to you in one way or another.
